Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

When can I expect a reply?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days.

How can I request account and data deletion?

If you wish to delete your user account and all associated data from any of our applications, please send us an email with the subject "Account Deletion Request" to info@koje.sk. We will process your request within 30 days.
